Sobre o Bazar Militar

Welcome to Habits! We're a team working to help students with habit tracking. Through our browser extension, students can add tasks that they want to complete throughout the day, ranging from mental wellness, skincare, reading for 10 minutes, drinking a glass of water, and much more. A to-do list allows you to easily keep track of what you have (and haven't) done, taking the stress of remembering off you.

Our extension also comes with suggestions for different tasks such as a 10 minute walk or stretches, rewarding you with a variety of awards to celebrate your achievements. Screen break alerts notify you when you've been looking at the screen for an extended period of time, allowing you to manage healthy breaks without having to mentally distract from your assignments.
